## What is Fiber Map?

Fiber Map tracks broadband infrastructure availability across the United States, analyzing internet service providers, coverage areas, and technology types.

## Data Architecture

The project follows a four-layer architecture:

- **CLEAN**: Raw data from source systems
- **STAGE**: Standardized data with cleaned column names
- **MODEL**: Normalized business entities with derived fields
- **GOLD**: Analysis-ready datasets

## Technology Stack

- **dbt Core**: Data transformation
- **Snowflake**: Data warehouse (`FIBER_MAP` database)
- **Lightdash**: Business intelligence

## Current Scope

- **Geography**: Indiana
- **Data**: Location-level broadband availability by provider and technology over time
